The Leage of Extaordinary Gentleman Game



Game Instructions


You will be giving a random number at the start of the game.

There are four league members below. By clicking on a league member you will add a spedific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number.


The value of each league member is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the league members.
